Turtle Categories

Sri Lanka is fortunate to have five of the seven sea turtle species that are still alive and have been identified as nesting along the island's stunning coastline.

The "big five" include the Leatherback (Dermochelys coriacea), Hawksbill (Eretmochelys imbricata), Loggerhead (Caretta caretta), Olive Ridley (Chelonia mydas), and Hawksbill (Lepidochelys olivacea).

Therefore, if you plan a beach vacation in Sri Lanka with the help of knowledgeable professionals like Mahoora Tented Camps Sri Lanka, you are almost certain to see some! (Manhoora, 2023)
